💡 The Idea
MoodAI was born from a simple observation — we try to be productive without considering how we actually feel. Emotions directly impact our performance, yet no tool accounted for that reality.
🛠️ The Build
Built with Python Flask on the backend, DeepFace for facial emotion recognition, and Claude AI for generating personalized text responses and activity suggestions tailored to each user.
🎯 The Mission
To help anyone — student, professional, or creator — align their daily actions with their emotional reality, resulting in less burnout, more focus, and more meaningful work every day.
